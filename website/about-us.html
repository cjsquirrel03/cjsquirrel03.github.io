About Us

We are located in the tippy-top of Alaska, in Atka. We were founded by Britney Spears when she noticed a xylophone and thought to herself she just had to have it. The museum has spread from there, hosting concerts and exhibitions examining all kinds of percussion instruments. We collect instruments from all over the world, and we helicopter them into the museum through the removable ceiling.
The Museum of Percussion Instruments (MoPI) enriches our world by collecting, preserving, and making accessible an astonishing variety of musical instruments and performance videos from every country in the world. MoPI offers guests a welcoming and fun experience, incomparable interactive technology, dynamic programming, and exceptional musical performances. MoPI fosters appreciation of the world’s diverse cultures by showing how we innovate, adapt, and learn from each other to create music—the language of the soul.
